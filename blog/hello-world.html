Hello, world

This blog is three hand-written HTML files and no build step. No generator, no Node, no CI to publish a paragraph. Writing a post means copying this file, changing the words, and adding one <li> to blog.html.

Why so plain

Every static-site generator I've run eventually needed feeding: a broken theme after an upgrade, a plugin that stopped building, a lockfile that drifted. For a handful of posts that cost is bigger than the posts. When the count of posts outgrows my patience for hand-editing an index, I'll switch to something like Zola or mdBook. Not before.

That's the whole system. It fits in your head, which is the point.
